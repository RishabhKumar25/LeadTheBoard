About The Contest

Lead the Board is a weekly coding contest of Department of AIT-CSE of Chandigarh University.

This coding arena will help students to add the coding habit in their daily life and it will lead to achieve the coding consistency of students.

Benifits

Cause is an essential part of anything.

Habit of being consistent in coding

Weekly coding contest will help students to practice coding constitently.

Stand out from others

Lead the Board program is designed in such a way that it feature the top scorer of the week in the whole department.

Competitive coding mindset

The leaderboard will help students to develop a competitive mindset and it will create a hunger to perform better.

Rules

Defining the rules can lead to success of a program.


4 Contests in a month (Weekly)

There will be maximum 4 weekly contests in a month as a month contains 4 weeks in major term

Contest portal will open for only 3 days in a week

Friday, Saturday and Sunday are the weekdays on which the contest portal will be open for that particular week contest

Leaderboard Announcement

Leaderboard will be displayed in every classrom of AIT-CSE department on monday. Result will be containing the data of top scorer of recent week contest.

Top coder of the month - Certificate

A certificate will be given to the top scorer of the month. Final monthly score will be the average of scores of all weekly contest of that month. Based on that average score the topper of the leaderboard will get a certificate after a evaluation process.

Evaluation process before certificate

A small one to one evaluation will be done of the topper of the leaderboard, if the participant passes the process then they will get the certificate otherwise the second top scorer will get the chance of evaluation and this process will go untill our jury get the deserving participant

Jury's decision will be final

Decision of jury member will be final.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Student of AIT-CSE department of Chandigarh University can participate in this program.

Maximum 4 coding contest in a month will be organised in this program.

Friday to Sunday are the days of that particular week on which the contest portal will be live.

Weekly coding contest leaderboard will be displayed in each class room of AIT CSE department on monday and the monthly leaderboard will be based on the average score of weekly contest.

After a evaluation process top coder of the month will get a certificate for the same.
